Prototype notes

  • The cardboard housing keeps the phone display within the user's line of sight.
  • An IR sensor detects when hands move near the sink to start/stop the timer.
  • Visual feedback shifts from calm greens to urgent reds to discourage water waste.
  • Summary cards rotate every 5 seconds to avoid overwhelming the user.
  • A QR hand-off lets users keep their personal conservation stats.
